Real-time data

Distance should not obstruct a shared experience.

Jiří works on infrastructure and applications for ultra-low-latency audiovisual transmission. At SAGElab, he connects networking, large-scale visualisation, the Internet of Things and live culture.

01

Low latency

Delay-sensitive video transmission, network path measurement and systems for synchronous remote collaboration.

02

Networked culture

Technical facilities for distributed performances where musicians and performers collaborate across cities and continents.

03

Internet of Things

Monitoring sensitive objects, a dedicated LoRaWAN testbed and connecting sensors with visualisation environments.

Experimental environment

Network, image and space as one system.

Research infrastructure makes it possible to validate technologies in real cultural, educational and scientific scenarios.
