Model, image and space

A mathematical foundation for visual and immersive technologies.

Petr connects applied mathematics with computer graphics. His work covers virtual and mixed reality, medical image data and numerical models in materials science.

01

Virtual reality

Headset technology, motion tracking, spatial interaction and immersive application development.

02

Imaging data

Medical data visualisation and segmentation, geometric representations and image analysis.

03

Numerical models

Computational fluid dynamics, evolving curves and process simulations in materials science.
