Object, model, space

A digital model should be accurate and accessible.

Jiří works on acquiring 3D data through photogrammetry, processing it and presenting it interactively. At SAGElab, he connects large-scale visualisation with applications for culture, education and remote collaboration.

01

Photogrammetry

Documenting real objects, preparing image data and producing usable 3D representations.

02

Interactive 3D

Web model viewers, annotations, synchronised control and access from different devices.

03

Large-scale visualisation

SAGE and SAGE2, display walls and sharing extensive visual data in a common space.

Practical environment

3D content from capture to a shared viewing session.

Tool development follows the needs of museums, research teams and institutions that make image and spatial data accessible.
