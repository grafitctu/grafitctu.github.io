Research, teaching and making

From image algorithms to interactive worlds.

Radek connects technical research in computer graphics and pattern recognition with game design, teaching and public presentation of student work. He leads GRAFIT, ggLab and the development of the VCGD master's specialisation.

Images and textures

Dynamic textures, contextual image completion, recognition and material appearance modelling.

02

Games and engines

Game design, interactive world architecture, prototyping and supervision of student game projects.

03

Cultural heritage

Digital reconstruction, historical imagery and translating visual sources into 3D and VR.
