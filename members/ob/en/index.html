Observe, understand, improve

A design decision needs evidence.

Ondřej works in user experience research and designs processes that translate observations of real people into tangible improvements to an application, service or learning environment.

01

User research

Interviews, usability testing and qualitative analysis of users' needs, problems and decision strategies.

02

03

Project courses, physical and digital prototypes and new ways to involve students in solving real problems.
