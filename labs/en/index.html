How to start

A project, a question or curiosity is enough.

The labs are open to students, researchers and industry partners. The fastest route is through the contact person at the relevant laboratory.

1

Choose a topic

Graphics and games, UX, VR or a combination of several areas.

2

Describe the idea

Briefly tell us what you are building, for whom and how far along it is.

3

Arrange support

A consultation, user study, equipment loan or collaboration.

4

Validate the result

Show the prototype to users, the community or at a student event.

Not sure where to go?

Start with the GRAFIT community.

Discord is the fastest way to find someone working on a related topic, arrange a consultation and identify suitable equipment.
